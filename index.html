01 — About

I'm an Information Design and Technology student who split the difference between construction sites and design software, and met in the middle of technology and design.

My work moves between immersive digital experiences, architecture, and branding, with a quiet interest for building software that makes complicated information easy to hold. Before design school, I spent years translating between languages in a legal practice, which taught me that the best interfaces, like the best translations, disappear.
